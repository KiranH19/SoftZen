Common questions

Everything you need to know.

How long does a typical project take?

Simple websites take 2–3 weeks. Complex web applications or AI integrations typically run 6–12 weeks. We'll give you a detailed timeline in your project scope document before work begins.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely. We work asynchronously with clients across India, the US, UK, and the Middle East. All communication is in English and we adapt to your timezone for live calls.

What does the AI chatbot integration look like?

We typically build on top of GPT-4 or similar LLMs, trained on your product documentation, FAQs, and policies. The chatbot can handle customer queries, qualify leads, or book appointments — depending on your use case.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance and support?

Yes. Every package includes post-launch support, and we offer monthly retainer agreements for businesses that need ongoing updates, content, analytics, and feature development.

Can you redesign an existing website?

Yes — in fact, redesigns are one of our specialities. We audit your current site, identify conversion and performance gaps, and rebuild with measurable improvement goals.
